Fibre: Thin thread-like structure obtained from plants or animals.

Types of Fibres:

  • Natural Fibre (Cotton, Wool)
  • Synthetic Fibre (Nylon, Polyester)

Cotton: Obtained from cotton plant.

Wool: Obtained from sheep hair.

Process of Making Fabric:

  • Ginning
  • Spinning
  • Weaving
  • Knitting

üìù Short Questions

1. What is fibre?
Answer: Thin thread-like structure.

2. Name two natural fibres.
Answer: Cotton and Wool.

3. What is spinning?
Answer: Making yarn from fibres.

üìï Long Question

Explain process of making fabric.
Cotton is separated by ginning, spun into yarn, then woven or knitted to make fabric.
